Alhambra Palace

The Alhambra is a stunning palace and fortress complex in Granada, Spain.

It's celebrated for its Islamic architecture, gardens, and history.

And that's why I also love it so much!

The Alhambra is a stunning blend of masterclass design, intricate artistry, and environmental harmony

Overview

  1. Full Name: Alhambra Palace and Fortress
  2. Location: Granada, Andalusia, Spain
  3. Original Builders: Nasrid dynasty
  4. Purpose: Royal palace, military fortress

Style: Islamic (Moorish) architecture

Design Features:

  • Palaces: intricate stucco work
  • Court of the Lions
  • Generalife gardens
  • Fortress (Alcazaba)
  • Hall of the Ambassadors
