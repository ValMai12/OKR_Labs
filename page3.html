Sagrada Familia

One of the most iconic landmarks is the Sagrada Familia

in Barcelona, designed by Antoni Gaudí.

It is a Roman Catholic church and also a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Sagrada Familia Street View

Overview

  1. Basílica i Temple Expiatori
  2. Location: Barcelona
  3. Architect: Antoni Gaudí
  4. Construction Started: 1882

Style: Gothic + Modernisme

Design Features:

  • Intricate facades
  • 18 towers
  • Tree-like columns
  • Stained glass windows
